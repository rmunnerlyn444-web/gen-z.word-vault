Rizz

Short for "charisma." Used when someone has strong flirting skills or smooth energy.

Example: "Bro pulled up with crazy rizz and got her number instantly."

Simp

Someone who goes above and beyond for someone they like, often without receiving much back.

Example: "He bought her flowers every week. Man’s a simp."

Cap

A lie or exaggeration. "No cap" means "no lie."

Example: "You said you benched 300? That’s cap."

W

Slang for "win." Used to praise something good or successful.

Example: "That new song? Straight W."

F

Respect or acknowledgment of something unfortunate, from gaming meme "Press F to pay respects."

Example: "His WiFi cut out during finals. F in the chat."
